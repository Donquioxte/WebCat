How I work

What every engagement includes

A few things that are standard on every engagement, not optional extras.

Signed NDA before testing
A mutual NDA and written authorisation letter are in place before any testing begins. No exceptions.
One free retest included
All findings are retested after remediation at no extra charge, within 30 days of original report delivery.
Executive + technical report
Every report includes a plain-language executive summary alongside the full technical findings — written for both stakeholders and engineers.
Debrief call included
A walkthrough call after report delivery to answer questions, clarify findings, and help your team prioritise what gets fixed first.
48-hour report turnaround
Reports are delivered within 48 hours of testing completion. You won't be waiting weeks for findings you needed last week.
Full confidentiality
Findings, client details, and all engagement data are handled with strict confidentiality. Nothing is shared without explicit permission.
