Web app pen-test
From $1,500
5 – 10 business days

A manual, in-depth assessment of your web application targeting authentication flaws, injection vulnerabilities, business logic errors, broken access controls, and API weaknesses. Goes well beyond automated scanning.

What's included

  • Authentication & session management testing

Network pen-test
From $2,000
5 – 15 business days

External and internal infrastructure testing to identify exploitable misconfigurations, weak credentials, unpatched services, and lateral movement paths. Covers both perimeter and internal network depending on scope.

What's included
  • External perimeter assessment
  • Internal network testing (on-site or via VPN)
  • Service enumeration and vulnerability identification
  • Firewall and ACL review
  • Lateral movement and privilege escalation testing
  • Prioritised remediation report and retest
Social engineering
From $800
3 – 7 business days

Simulated phishing and pretexting campaigns to measure your team's susceptibility to human-layer attacks. Results are used to identify training gaps, not to penalise staff.

What's included
  • Custom phishing campaign design and execution
  • Spear-phishing targeting key roles
  • Credential harvesting simulation (no real data stored)
  • Click-rate and reporting metrics per department
  • Awareness training recommendations
Red teaming
From $5,000
3 – 6 weeks

A full-scope adversary simulation targeting your people, processes, and technology simultaneously. Designed to test your detection and response capabilities as much as your defences. Coordinated with a trusted internal contact only.

What's included
  • Initial reconnaissance and OSINT gathering
  • Multi-vector attack simulation (phishing, network, physical if agreed)
  • Persistence and lateral movement
  • Objective-based testing (data exfiltration, domain takeover etc.)
  • Full attack narrative and timeline report
  • Detection gap analysis and blue team debrief
